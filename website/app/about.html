About Witty

Today's e-commerce sites are awesome but they miss few important things like -- Seller-User Interaction ,Flexibilities in prices , negotiating , user insights for seller. Witty is a step towards making e-commerce more real world like.

So to fight all the evils of e-commerce , Witty is born! We basically aim at

  • 1.Bridging the gap between seller and user
  • 2.Increasing competition bw sellers and thereby reducing price
  • 3.Simulating a real world market like e-commerce where buyers and sellers have more flexibility in terms of prices and products.
  • 4.Making e-commerce browsing more interactive.

Walkthrough of the site

.

  • Open view as Seller in one tab and View as seller on another tab.
  • Select category , search and sort the results as you want.
  • If you don't find the product you are looking for , Click Shout Out in the Navigation bar.
  • Or if you get desired prodcut try requesting a bargain or add to cart on any product and You'll see the count to be update in navigation bar.
  • If you requested a bargain You can see at sellers Dashboard an active bargain request.
  • Try texting the user , offering him , accepting , e.t.c. and see the changes in the bargian status at user's side.
  • Accept,Deny offers at the user's side and see the changes in the cart.
  • As a seller view various stats like history , product Insights , user Demands ,e.t.c
